Our Story

Born From a Love of
Remarkable Journeys

WanderLux was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate travellers who believed that every person deserves a perfectly crafted travel experience — not just a booking. We grew tired of generic packages and impersonal service, so we built something better.

Starting with just a handful of handpicked Indian destinations, we've grown to curate travel experiences across 32+ destinations worldwide — from the misty hills of Coorg to the caldera cliffs of Santorini. Every listing, every stay, every vehicle on our platform is personally reviewed by our team.

Today, over 15,000 happy travellers have experienced the WanderLux difference — and we're just getting started.

Our Mission

To make world-class travel experiences accessible, affordable and deeply personal for every Indian traveller.

Our Vision

A world where every journey is transformative — not transactional. We exist to craft memories, not just book trips.
