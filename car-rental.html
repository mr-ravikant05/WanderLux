Popular Driving Routes

Legendary road trips that every traveller must experience at least once in their lifetime.

Manali to Leh Highway

One of the world's highest motorable roads cutting through Rohtang Pass, Baralacha La and the Sarchu plains — pure epic.

480 km 2–3 days Best: Jun–Sep

Mumbai to Goa Coastal

The legendary NH66 — winding coastal road past Kashid, Ganpatipule, Ganapatipule with ocean views every few kilometres.

590 km 1–2 days Best: Oct–Mar

Delhi to Jaipur–Udaipur

The classic Rajasthan road trip — forts, palaces, camel safaris and endless desert sunsets on smooth NH48.

630 km circuit 3–5 days Best: Oct–Feb

Bangalore to Coorg–Ooty

Scenic Western Ghats drive through coffee and spice estates, misty valleys and charming Kodava culture.

420 km loop 2–3 days Best: Sep–Feb

Spiti Valley Circuit

Shimla to Kaza to Manali via Pin Valley — stark raw landscapes, ancient monasteries and river crossings.

900 km loop 7–10 days Best: Jun–Oct

Kerala Coastal Drive

Kochi to Kovalam along the Arabian Sea coast — backwater bridges, fishing villages and golden beach sunsets.

360 km 2–3 days Best: Nov–Mar

Guwahati to Tawang Escape

A breathtaking Himalayan journey through mountain passes, monasteries and misty valleys.

520 km 3–4 days Best: Mar–Jun

Ahmedabad to Kutch White Desert

Drive across Gujarat to the surreal white salt desert, with vibrant villages and stunning sunsets.

400 km 2–3 days Best: Nov–Feb
