Plan Smart

Best Seasons to Travel

Know when to go for the perfect weather, fewer crowds and best prices.

☀️

Summer (Mar–Jun)

Perfect for hill stations — Shimla, Manali, Darjeeling, Coorg. Escape the plains heat in the cool Himalayas.

🌧️

Monsoon (Jul–Sep)

Lush Kerala, Coorg waterfalls, and the dramatic Sahyadri Ghats come alive. Ideal for nature seekers.

🍂

Autumn (Oct–Nov)

The golden season — Rajasthan, Varanasi, and the Himalayas shine. Festivals, clear skies, perfect weather.

❄️

Winter (Dec–Feb)

Goa, Andaman, Kerala beaches peak. International — Santorini, Bali, Maldives. Ladakh for snow lovers.

Insider Knowledge

Essential Travel Tips

From our seasoned travel experts — advice that makes every trip smoother.

01

Book at least 30 days in advance for peak-season destinations like Goa, Manali and Andaman to get the best rates.

02

Carry a travel insurance policy — it protects against trip cancellation, medical emergencies and lost baggage.

03

For international trips, apply for your visa at least 4–6 weeks before departure. Some destinations offer on-arrival visas.

04

Pack light and smart — a 20L carry-on is ideal for trips under 7 days. Use packing cubes to organise efficiently.

05

Download offline maps (Google Maps, Maps.me) and save emergency contacts before entering remote destinations like Spiti or Ladakh.

06

Respect local customs and dress codes, especially at religious sites in Rajasthan, Varanasi, Kyoto and Bali.

Recommended Experiences

Houseboat Nights

Drift through Kerala backwaters on a traditional kettuvallam, watching fireflies over the lagoon.

Himalayan Treks

Trek Triund, Kedarkantha or the Hampta Pass — legendary routes suited for all fitness levels.

Culinary Tours

Savour authentic street food in Varanasi, coastal seafood in Goa and ramen in Tokyo's tiny alleys.

Sunrise Shoots

Capture the golden hour at Tiger Hill (Darjeeling), Taj Mahal and Santorini's caldera cliffs.

Island Hopping

Explore Andaman's Havelock, Neil and Baratang islands by speedboat for a complete tropical adventure.

Wellness Retreats

Rejuvenate with Ayurvedic treatments in Kerala, yoga in Rishikesh and healing spas in Ubud.
